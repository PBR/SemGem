Semantic Germplasm

Prototype application to show the potential for integration breeding database with genebank passport, characterization and evaluation data.

This appliction uses semantic web technology to aggregate information from both resources. The integration is currently only possible with EU-SOL records and records from the genebank of the Centre For Genetic Resources (CGN), Wageningen UR, The Netherlands.

Valid EU-SOL accessions, with corresponding records from the CGN genebank are from EA01895 till EA02055.
